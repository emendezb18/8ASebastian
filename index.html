The Cold War

One of the Wars that Marked History

By: Lucas Garcia, Luciana Africano, & Sebastian Espinel


Beginnings of the Cold War

The Cold War started on March 12, 1947, it was a conflict between the United States and the Soviet Union. The Cold War ended on December 25, 1991, it ended with the surrender and separation of the Soviet Union where 15 new countries were born off its corpse.
